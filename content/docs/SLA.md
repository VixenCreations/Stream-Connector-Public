and **VixForge Haptics Nexus**. Each app is licensed independently; the terms below apply per-app and

> model that is not yet final, and Stream Connector is not yet available for purchase. Support tiers,
> response targets, and remedies may be adjusted before launch. The version in effect at the time of
> your purchase is authoritative.

> **Local-first by design.** Both apps run entirely on your own machine. There is no hosted backend
> that stores your data or runs your setup, so this SLA does **not** promise uptime for a cloud service
> you do not depend on. Instead, it covers the two things we actually operate - **license validation**
> and **update distribution** - plus our **support responsiveness**.

## Scope of coverage

**Covered:**

- **License validation.** Activation and periodic re-checks for paid licenses (processed through our
  store provider, Gumroad).
- **Update distribution.** Availability of update downloads and release artifacts we publish.
- **Support responsiveness.** The initial-response and triage targets defined below, by tier.

**Not covered:**

- Your hardware, local network, or operating system.
- Third-party services and integrations (VRChat, Intiface, PiShock, OwO, Streamer.bot, TikTok,
  Discord, and Gumroad), which are governed by their own terms and availability.
- Misconfiguration by the user, or features explicitly marked experimental or beta.
- The moment-to-moment operation of the app on your machine, which runs locally and is not a service
  we host.

## Severity definitions

- **Severity 1 - Critical.** Core functionality unusable, activation or update distribution down for a
  paid user, or a safety-relevant issue.
- **Severity 2 - High.** Significant feature degradation affecting many users; a major workflow is
  blocked but a workaround exists.
- **Severity 3 - Medium.** Partial loss of functionality; single-user impact or a non-critical feature
  broken.
- **Severity 4 - Low.** Cosmetic issues, documentation requests, feature requests, or general
  questions.

## License validation and update availability

Because the apps run locally, they keep working during a temporary outage of our licensing or update
services:

- **Activation grace period.** After a successful activation, a paid license continues to operate
  offline for a grace period between successful online re-checks, so a brief validation outage does
  not stop a licensed user from working. The app re-verifies when a connection is available.
- **Best-effort availability.** We operate license validation and update distribution on a best-effort
  basis and work to keep planned downtime short and announced. We do not commit to a fixed uptime
  percentage for these services, and third-party providers we rely on (such as Gumroad) are outside
  our control.

## Incident communication

- **Reporting.** Open a ticket via email Vixenlicious@outlook.com or the priority Discord channel (Pro and
  Lifetime). Include timestamps, logs, and reproduction steps.
- **Status updates.** For a Severity 1 incident affecting our licensing or update services, we will
  post status updates until mitigation. For Severity 2 incidents we will update periodically until
  resolved.
- **Post-incident summary.** For a Severity 1 incident affecting paid subscribers, a written summary
  will be provided within 7 business days.

---

## Remedies

If an outage of **our** license-validation or update-distribution services (not your hardware,
network, or a third-party provider) prevents a paid user from using the Software for an extended
period, the affected account may request a discretionary service credit. Submit a ticket with relevant
logs within 14 days. Service credits are the sole remedy under this SLA. Lifetime holders receive
on-call escalation and prioritized engineering support for critical incidents in place of recurring
credits.

---

## Escalation path

1. **Support** - initial triage and workaround.
2. **Senior engineering** - deeper diagnostics and patch coordination.
3. **On-call lead** - engaged for Severity 1; automatic for Lifetime customers, by escalation for Pro.
4. **Owner** - final escalation for unresolved critical issues.

Include your ticket ID in all communications.

## Maintenance

Planned maintenance to our licensing or update services will be scheduled during low-traffic windows
and announced in advance via Discord and email where practical. Planned maintenance does not count as
an incident.

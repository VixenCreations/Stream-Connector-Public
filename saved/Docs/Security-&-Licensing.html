Security & Licensing

Two editions, one license model - so let's be precise.

Stream Connector (the full edition) and VixForge Haptics Nexus (the haptics-focused edition) are built from the same engine and share the same single-user license model: each has a feature-limited free tier plus paid Pro and Lifetime tiers.

A paid license requires a one-time activation - a purchase email + license key - after which the app runs locally like everything else. The two editions are licensed and billed separately. Stream Connector is not yet available for purchase, and its pricing is provisional and subject to change at release.

License Model

Both editions are paid products under the same single-user license model, each with a feature-limited free tier plus paid Pro and Lifetime tiers.

  • Activate a paid tier once with your purchase email + license key
  • Credentials stored encrypted and locally (machine-bound)
  • Keeps working offline within a grace window between online re-checks
  • No refunds (digital product) - full terms are provided with your purchase

Either way, it runs on your machine, under your control.

Security Philosophy

Security here is boring by design - because boring security is reliable security.

  • Local execution only
  • No background services
  • No cloud dependency
  • No telemetry
  • No forced updates

Nothing runs without you launching it. Nothing hides what it's doing.

Licensing note: the free tier never phones home. A paid license makes exactly one kind of outbound call for licensing - it contacts the Gumroad license API to verify your key at activation and periodically afterward. It sends only your license key and purchase email for that check. No telemetry, no analytics, no usage tracking.

Network & Runtime Behavior

  • Explicit WebSocket connections only
  • No external listeners unless enabled
  • All ports and endpoints are visible
  • Fails closed, not open

If it’s talking to something, you configured it.

Antivirus & Trust

All builds are scanned and validated through:

  • Windows Defender cloud reputation checks
  • Standard executable trust heuristics

There is no telemetry submission. There is no remote scanning. There is no hidden updater.

If your antivirus complains, it’s because this software:

  • Controls hardware
  • Opens sockets
  • Does real work
  • Is designed to real time communicate with interconnected software channels

Not because it’s malicious.

What This Does NOT Do

  • No data collection
  • No analytics
  • No accounts
  • No cloud login
  • No remote kill switches

Licensing note: the one exception is paid-license validation - a purchase that is refunded, charged back, or disputed will fail its license re-check and the paid tier will stop operating. That is license enforcement, not telemetry or a remote kill switch on your data or your machine.

You own your install. You own your data. You own the consequences.

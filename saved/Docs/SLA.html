Service Level Agreement

Last Updated: 07-07-2026  •  Provider: VixForge Interactive

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) defines the support and service commitments for Stream Connector and VixForge Haptics Nexus. Each app is licensed independently; the terms below apply per-app and per-account. For pricing and tiers see the Pricing page.

This SLA is provisional and subject to change at release. It is published alongside a pricing model that is not yet final, and Stream Connector is not yet available for purchase. Support tiers, response targets, and remedies may be adjusted before launch. The version in effect at the time of your purchase is authoritative.
Local-first by design. Both apps run entirely on your own machine. There is no hosted backend that stores your data or runs your setup, so this SLA does not promise uptime for a cloud service you do not depend on. It covers the two things we actually operate - license validation and update distribution - plus our support responsiveness.

Scope of Coverage

Covered:

  • License validation - activation and periodic re-checks for paid licenses (processed through our store provider, Gumroad).
  • Update distribution - availability of update downloads and release artifacts we publish.
  • Support responsiveness - the initial-response and triage targets defined below, by tier.

Not covered:

  • Your hardware, local network, or operating system.
  • Third-party services and integrations (VRChat, Intiface, PiShock, OwO, Streamer.bot, TikTok, Discord, and Gumroad), governed by their own terms and availability.
  • Misconfiguration by the user, or features explicitly marked experimental or beta.
  • The moment-to-moment operation of the app on your machine, which runs locally and is not a service we host.

Support Tiers and Channels

  • Free Tier - community forum and public Discord channels. No guaranteed response times.
  • Pro (monthly or annual) - priority Discord support and email Vixenlicious@outlook.com, with the guaranteed initial-response targets below.
  • Lifetime - email Vixenlicious@outlook.com plus on-call escalation for critical incidents, with the elevated targets below.

Severity Definitions

  • Severity 1 - Critical. Core functionality unusable, activation or update distribution down for a paid user, or a safety-relevant issue.
  • Severity 2 - High. Significant feature degradation affecting many users; a major workflow is blocked but a workaround exists.
  • Severity 3 - Medium. Partial loss of functionality; single-user impact or a non-critical feature broken.
  • Severity 4 - Low. Cosmetic issues, documentation requests, feature requests, or general questions.

These are response targets, not resolution guarantees. Resolution time depends on the issue's complexity, reproducibility, and the fix required. For Severity 1 incidents we commit to working continuously until a mitigation or workaround is in place.

License Validation and Update Availability

Because the apps run locally, they keep working during a temporary outage of our licensing or update services:

  • Activation grace period. After a successful activation, a paid license continues to operate offline for a grace period between successful online re-checks, so a brief validation outage does not stop a licensed user from working. The app re-verifies when a connection is available.
  • Best-effort availability. We operate license validation and update distribution on a best-effort basis and work to keep planned downtime short and announced. We do not commit to a fixed uptime percentage for these services, and third-party providers we rely on (such as Gumroad) are outside our control.

Incident Communication

  • Reporting. Open a ticket via email Vixenlicious@outlook.com or the priority Discord channel (Pro and Lifetime). Include timestamps, logs, and reproduction steps.
  • Status updates. For a Severity 1 incident affecting our licensing or update services, we will post status updates until mitigation. For Severity 2 incidents we will update periodically until resolved.
  • Post-incident summary. For a Severity 1 incident affecting paid subscribers, a written summary will be provided within 7 business days.

Remedies

If an outage of our license-validation or update-distribution services (not your hardware, network, or a third-party provider) prevents a paid user from using the Software for an extended period, the affected account may request a discretionary service credit. Submit a ticket with relevant logs within 14 days. Service credits are the sole remedy under this SLA. Lifetime holders receive on-call escalation and prioritized engineering support for critical incidents in place of recurring credits.

Escalation Path

  1. Support - initial triage and workaround.
  2. Senior engineering - deeper diagnostics and patch coordination.
  3. On-call lead - engaged for Severity 1; automatic for Lifetime customers, by escalation for Pro.
  4. Owner - final escalation for unresolved critical issues.

Include your ticket ID in all communications.

Maintenance

Planned maintenance to our licensing or update services will be scheduled during low-traffic windows and announced in advance via Discord and email where practical. Planned maintenance does not count as an incident.

How to Request Support

  • Pro: priority Discord channel or email Vixenlicious@outlook.com. Include the app name, license email, severity, and reproduction steps.
  • Lifetime: email Vixenlicious@outlook.com with "ON-CALL ESCALATION" in the subject for critical incidents; include license details and incident data.
  • Free: community forum and public Discord channels.

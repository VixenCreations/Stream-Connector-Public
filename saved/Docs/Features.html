🫦 SPS / OGB Live-Touch

This is the one that changes everything.

VRChat tells the app where and how much you're being touched (SPS / OGB contacts). Stream Connector turns that into live device drive - in real time, scaled to the touch.

  • Touch harder and everything ramps. Let go and everything stops.
  • Drives ALL enabled outputs at once: Intiface intensity, OSC parameters, PiShock vibrate, and the OwO vest.
  • Continuous mode scales each output to the live touch level.
  • Threshold mode fires the whole chain once when the touch crosses the line you set.
  • Per-zone include/exclude and contact-type selection - who is allowed to set you off, and how.
  • PiShock stays VIBRATE on live touch and is never live-shocked. On purpose.

Get touched in game. Feel it on your hardware. That's the whole point.

Pricing

Last Updated: 07-07-2026  •  Provider: VixForge Interactive

Public pricing and licensing reference for Stream Connector and VixForge Haptics Nexus. Both are built from the same engine and sold as separate sister applications with independent licenses, billing, and support.

Pricing is provisional and subject to change at release. The figures below reflect the planned pricing model. Stream Connector is not yet available for purchase - its store listings and activation are still being finalized, and prices, tiers, and inclusions may change before launch. Nothing here is an offer or a binding quote; the store listing at time of purchase is authoritative.

For license grants and rules see the Security & Licensing page; for support commitments see the Service Level Agreement.

The Free Tier is feature-limited. Pro Monthly includes full feature access and priority support. Annual billing saves two months versus monthly. Tier Definitions

Free Tier

  • Purpose: try core functionality with limits on device count and advanced routing.
  • Includes: basic event ingestion or basic actuation, community support, and limited integrations.
  • Support: community channels only; no guaranteed response SLA.

Pro Monthly - $9.99 / month per app

  • Purpose: full access for active creators who want continuous updates and fast support.
  • Includes: the full feature set, continuous API and integration updates, automatic minor updates and security patches, priority Discord support with a guaranteed response SLA, and roadmap feedback channels.

Pro Annual - $99 / year per app

  • Purpose: cost-effective subscription for regular users.
  • Includes: all Pro Monthly benefits, billed annually - roughly two months free versus monthly.

Lifetime - $199 one-time per app

  • Purpose: a one-time purchase for creators who prefer owning a perpetual license.
  • Includes: a perpetual license for the purchased major engine version, minor updates and security patches, on-call support escalation for critical issues, and major upgrades as specified in the Lifetime terms.

Licensing and Support

  • Separate licenses. Stream Connector and Haptics Nexus are licensed independently. Subscriptions are per-account and per-app. Lifetime licenses are perpetual for the purchased major engine version.
  • Updates. Subscriptions include continuous API and integration maintenance. Lifetime licenses include minor updates and security patches; major architectural upgrades are included as specified in the Lifetime terms.
  • Support. Free Tier uses community channels. Pro includes priority Discord support with a guaranteed response SLA. Lifetime holders receive on-call escalation for critical issues. Targets are defined in the SLA.
  • Refunds and transfers. All sales are final. Because these are digital products delivered instantly, purchases are non-refundable once delivered, and cancelling a subscription stops future charges but does not refund payments already made. Full terms are on the store listing and in the License & Usage Terms. License transfers, where offered, are handled via sales contact.
  • Trial status. A trial version is planned and will be announced when implemented.

FAQ

Yes. Stream Connector and Haptics Nexus are sold separately, each at its own price.

What does Lifetime cover?
Perpetual use of the purchased major engine version, minor updates, and security patches; on-call escalation for critical issues; and major upgrades as specified in the Lifetime terms.

Is there a trial?
A trial is planned but not yet implemented. Details will be published when it is available.

When can I buy Stream Connector?
Stream Connector is not yet available for purchase. Its store listings are being finalized, and the pricing on this page is provisional until launch.

How do I get support?
Pro subscribers receive priority Discord support with a guaranteed response SLA. Lifetime holders receive on-call escalation for critical issues. See the SLA.
